Stage-Gate® Gate 1 Evaluation Worksheet

Gate 1 is a quick screen to decide if an opportunity is worth exploring further.

How to use this worksheet

Fill in the sections below. Keep your writing short and clear. This is a first draft, not a final report.

Gate 1 key question: Is this opportunity worth investigating further?

Tip for Week 1: “Market size potential” is an early evidence check. You are not doing full market sizing yet.

Project information

The organisation or brand you are evaluating
What category is this opportunity in?
A short name for your idea direction
Summarise the opportunity in two or three sentences
Choose the main group this serves

Gate 1 must-meet criteria

These must be satisfied to proceed. Mark each as pass or fail, then explain why.

Does this opportunity fit the organisation’s brand, strengths, and direction?
One or two short sentences. What makes it a good fit (or not)?
Early check: is there enough evidence this problem affects enough people to matter?
Add one source and one sentence explaining why the opportunity is meaningful.
Could the organisation realistically build and deliver this?
Think about operations, supply chain, partners, safety, and compliance (if relevant).

Gate 1 should-meet criteria

Rate each criterion from one to five. One is poor, three is adequate, five is excellent.

How different could this be compared to what customers already use?
How well does this use what the organisation already has (skills, channels, partners, brand trust)?
Two or three short sentences. Why did you choose those ratings?

Final comments

Write your decision, a short reason, one risk, and one next step.
